The Fraser Institutes 'Economics Freedom Score': A Measure for Regulation

Low levels of regulation promote higher 'Economic Freedom Scores', we can see this scores trends in the G20 over time. There are a few clear leaders, with the US almost always at the top. Despite this, we have seen Economic Freedom ratings in the G20 trend downwards over the last 20 years, matching the trends that we have seen in regulation levels.
